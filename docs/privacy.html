Privacy Policy

Photo Batch Importer · Last updated: June 4, 2026

Photo Batch Importer does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All photo and video processing happens entirely on your Mac.

What the app accesses

Photo Batch Importer reads photos and videos from memory cards and folders you explicitly choose, and copies them to a destination folder you select. It reads image metadata (such as capture date and camera model) only to name and organize your files. This information never leaves your Mac and is never sent to the developer or any third party.

Data we collect

None. The app contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. We have no servers and receive no information about you or your use of the app.

iCloud

If you are signed in to iCloud, the app stores your own preferences — import options and saved naming presets — in Apple's iCloud key-value storage so they stay in sync across your Macs. This data is held in your private iCloud account, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, and is never accessible to the developer. Your photos are never uploaded to iCloud by this app.

Your photos

Your photos and videos are only ever copied between locations you choose on your own devices and drives. The app never uploads, transmits, or retains them anywhere else.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
