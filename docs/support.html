Photo Batch Importer — Support

Photo Batch Importer imports photos and videos from SD/CF cards and other drives, renaming and organizing them with EXIF-driven naming rules. Need help? Email jianfeng.lin@gmail.com and I'll get back to you.

Frequently asked questions

I inserted a card but no photos appear.

The first time you use a card, click the card, and click Grant Access button in the popup window. macOS requires your permission for the app to read a removable volume; after you grant it once, the app remembers it for that card.

Does the app upload or share my photos?

No. All importing happens locally on your Mac. Photos are only copied between locations you choose. See our Privacy Policy.

How do naming templates work?

Each rule uses properties — date, time, camera model, lens, a sequence counter, card label, and more — to build folder and file names. Click a property to insert it, and watch the live "Destination" preview update before you import.

What about JPEG + RAW pairs?

Matching JPEG and RAW files from the same photo shot receive the same sequence number so they stay together.

Can it verify copies and delete from the card?

Yes. Select "Verify each file after copy" for a SHA-256 integrity check, and optionally delete from the card after import — always with a confirmation step first.

Do my settings sync between Macs?

If you're signed in to iCloud, your sequence counter, import options and saved presets sync automatically across your Macs.
